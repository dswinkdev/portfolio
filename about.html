About Me

Originally from Atlanta, Georgia (now based in Houston, Texas), I started my career in visual storytelling after earning an Associate of Applied Science in Web & Graphic Design. Over the past 10 years, I have established myself as a Senior Graphic Designer for Fortune 500 companies including Delta Air Lines, Yellow Pages (YP), Global Payments, and The Home Depot.

I’ve led brand campaigns, directed multi-channel creative efforts, and shaped the visual identity of some of the world's most recognized organizations. Alongside this, I have worked as a freelance Creative Director, helping startups and mid-sized companies craft compelling visual strategies that resonate with their audiences.

My passion for user experience and scalable solutions led me to pursue a career in technology. While my design career continues to thrive, I recently attended commencement at Southern New Hampshire University (SNHU), and I look forward to officially graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, concentrating in Software Engineering. My academic journey included immersive studies in Data Structures, Algorithms, Databases, and Object-Oriented Software Design, preparing me to make an impact at the intersection of creativity and engineering.

I have built full-stack applications and worked with Java, JavaScript, Node.js, MongoDB, SQL, and Android Studio, collaborating effectively in Agile teams. I am also UX/UI certified through Google’s professional certificate program and highly proficient with design systems and prototyping tools such as Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud.

Now, as a Creative Technologist and Full-Stack Software Engineer, I bridge the gap between design and engineering. I believe code should be as thoughtful and user-focused as the designs that inspire it. Whether building scalable web applications or crafting digital experiences from the ground up, I bring both an artist’s eye and an engineer’s mindset.

Core Competencies:

  • Creative Direction & Brand Identity
  • UI/UX Design (Certified – Google UX/UI Professional Certificate)
  • Front-End & Back-End Development (Java, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, Android Studio)
  • Visual Communication & Design Systems
  • Agile Development & Cross-functional Collaboration
  • Prototyping Tools: Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, InVision
  • Tech Stack: Java, Python, Git/GitHub, Firebase, REST APIs, SQL, JavaScript, MongoDB
  • Cloud & Data Foundations: AWS basics, database design, data structures & algorithms
  • Accessibility & Inclusive Design
