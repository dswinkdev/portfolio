Professional Self-Assessment

As I complete my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Southern New Hampshire University, I can confidently say this journey has transformed me. I began with a background in design and creativity but evolved into a multifaceted software engineer capable of building complete, user-centered systems.

Throughout this program, I developed proficiency in full-stack development, object-oriented programming, algorithm optimization, and mobile application development. My two featured artifacts—EvenTrack and Course Navigator Pro—exemplify how I’ve advanced technically and strategically in applying what I’ve learned to real-world projects.
